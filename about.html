About me

Wondering your life’s purpose is very much important, especially when you believe that you are born to be different. Hi, I’m Bhawan wadhwa, a traveler and a photographer from Punjab. Also, a believer of breaking the stereotypes society and creating my own tenacious life.

My goal is to show the beauty,grace,strength and enthusiasm of people and nature. I focus on capturing the beauty of nature and portray it to the world. I look for a sense of defiance and fortitude in my work.

Walking with pals, miles farther, spreading smiles make this beautiful journey. Letting my photographs speak and connecting hearts is what I always wish to see.
